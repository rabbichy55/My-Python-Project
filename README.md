### 1️⃣ Self-driving Car (Experimental Project)

# 🚗 Self-Driving Car Simulation

A simple self-driving car simulation built with Python and Pygame.
The car automatically follows a road by detecting pixel colors from the track image.

## 🧠 How It Works

The program checks pixel colors around the car to detect the road.

* **White (255)** → Road
* **Other colors** → Boundary / turn

Based on these values, the car decides to move forward or change direction.
